Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

How long does a typical project take? +
It depends entirely on scope. A simple landing site: 1–2 weeks. A mobile app: 8–12 weeks. A full SaaS platform: 3–6 months. We give you a precise timeline in our proposal after the discovery call.
Do you work with international clients? +
Yes — we have active clients in UAE, Germany, France, India, and the USA. We work async by default with weekly video calls. Time zone differences have never been a blocker.
Can you take over an existing codebase? +
Yes. We've inherited messy codebases many times. We start with an audit, document what exists, and propose a clean path forward — whether that's refactoring, extending, or a full rewrite with data migration.
What's your payment structure? +
Typically 40% upfront, 30% at mid-project milestone, 30% on delivery. For longer projects we use monthly retainers. We're flexible and can discuss what works for your situation.
Do you provide post-launch support? +
Yes. We offer 30 days of free bug-fix support post-launch for all projects. Ongoing retainers for feature development, monitoring, and maintenance are available and recommended for production systems.
Can you handle both design and development? +
Absolutely — and we prefer it. Having design and development under one roof means there's no translation loss between what was designed and what gets built. We've shipped many projects from wireframe to production.
