Who We Are

Engineers Who Actually Build Things.

Not a body shop. Not a middleman. A tight team of developers, designers, and hardware engineers who care deeply about what they ship.

Our Mission

We Build Systems That Outlast the Hype

Asiatec was founded on a simple frustration: too many tech agencies deliver polished demos that break in production. We do the opposite. Every system we ship — whether it's a Verilog FSM on Basys3 or a FastAPI backend serving 10,000 requests/day — is engineered to work under real conditions, not just in a presentation.

We work with startups who need to move fast, enterprises who need reliability, and researchers who need both. Our team writes the code, designs the circuits, edits the videos, and architects the databases — no outsourcing, no subcontracting.
